#en
configuration:
inside rapa_eth.py
RPC VAR => Your HTTP RPC
receiver Var => your address as receiver balance
arrayAcc Obj => Yours accounts privatekey in array with QM each and separated by , =>  "" ''

ex: arrayAcc = ['privateKey1', 'privateKey2'', 'privateKey3']
